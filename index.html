Ashley Thomas Barnes

I am post-doctoral researcher at the The Argelander-Institut für Astronomie (AIfA), University in Bonn. My research focus is on understanding the star formation process across the range of environments present within the Milky Way and beyond. In particular, I have lead and/or been involved in several works that aim at studying differences in the kinematic, chemical and excitation properties of molecular gas on the verge of forming stars within Galactic Centre and the Spiral Arm. These investigations made use of observations taken with a variety of telescopes (interferometric and single dish) over a range of wavelengths (millimetre, sub-millimetre radio to infrared).
